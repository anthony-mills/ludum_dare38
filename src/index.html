Kenny Cockroach

This game was made by Anthony Mills over the weekend of 22nd / 23rd April as an entry to the Ludum Dare game jam.

As such this game comes with no warranties implied or expressed. It probably contains a lot of bugs.

If the disclaimer wasn't enough to put you off, gameplay is simple. Help Kenny Cockroach find his way back to his roach motel using the cursor keys on your keyboard.

Avoid the spiders and the venom balls they shoot at all costs, as they believe Kenny Cockroach would provide a tasty feed.
